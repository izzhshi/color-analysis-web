Suggested Colors

For Accurate Results:

  • Use natural daylight for the best accuracy.
  • Avoid artificial lighting and harsh shadows.
  • Face the camera directly with your entire face visible.
  • Ensure a plain, neutral background (white or grey is best).
  • Remove makeup, glasses, and accessories to prevent interference.
  • Tie back hair to fully expose the forehead and face.
  • Wear neutral-colored clothing to avoid color reflection.
  • Stay still while capturing for a clear image.
